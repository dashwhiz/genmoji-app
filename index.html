Support

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Challenge Me?

Challenge Me gives you random puzzles from our library of 50+ pre-made challenges. The app tracks which ones you've solved, so you never see the same puzzle twice until you've completed them all!

How do I create a puzzle?

Tap "Create Puzzle", add 3-6 emoji using your iOS keyboard, fill in the title and answer, then save!

Where can I see my stats?

Your stats are displayed on the home screen! See how many puzzles you've created, solved, and skipped. Stats update automatically as you play.

How do hints work?

When playing a puzzle with a hint, tap the "Show Hint" button to reveal a clue. You can hide it again by tapping "Hide Hint".

What is Genmoji?

Genmoji are personalized emoji available on iOS 18.2+ devices with Apple Intelligence. The app works perfectly with standard emoji on all iOS 18+ devices!

How do I share puzzles?

Tap any puzzle from your library, then tap the "Share" button. You can share via Messages, social media, or any app!

Can I browse all puzzles?

Yes! Tap "Library" to see all 50+ pre-made puzzles. You can filter by category to find specific types of puzzles like Movies, Songs, or Food.

Is this really free?

Yes! No ads, no subscriptions, no in-app purchases.

Do I need an internet connection?

Nope! The app works 100% offline. Perfect for road trips and travel.

How do I delete a puzzle?

Swipe left on any puzzle in your library and tap "Delete".
